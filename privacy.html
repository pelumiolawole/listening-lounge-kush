Privacy Policy

The Listening Lounge by Kush
Last Updated: 22 May 2026

At The Listening Lounge by Kush, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our website, contact us, book a session, or engage with our services.

This policy has been adapted from the document you provided and updated to reflect current UK privacy expectations and the nature of our listening support services.

1. Who We Are

The Listening Lounge by Kush is a safe and supportive listening space where individuals can talk freely, vent, express emotions, and feel heard without judgement.

We provide:

  • Listening support
  • Emotional support conversations
  • Safe spaces to rant and express feelings
  • General wellbeing support

We do not provide clinical therapy or medical treatment.

For the purpose of UK data protection laws, The Listening Lounge by Kush acts as the data controller for your personal information.

2. Important Disclaimer

The Listening Lounge by Kush is a non-clinical listening service.

We are solely focused on:

  • Listening
  • Providing emotional support
  • Offering a safe and confidential space for people to rant, vent, and talk openly

We are not:

  • A therapy practice
  • A counselling service
  • A psychiatric or psychological service
  • A medical provider
  • A crisis intervention service

Our services should not be considered a replacement for:

  • Professional therapy
  • Counselling
  • Mental health treatment
  • Medical advice
  • Emergency support

If you are experiencing:

  • Severe emotional distress
  • Thoughts of self-harm
  • Suicidal thoughts
  • A mental health crisis
  • A medical emergency

Please contact:

  • Emergency services
  • Your GP
  • A licensed therapist or counsellor
  • Relevant crisis support services immediately

By using our services, you acknowledge and understand the nature and limitations of the support we provide.

3. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Telephone number
  • Booking information
  • Payment details where applicable
  • Messages or enquiries you send to us
  • Information voluntarily shared during conversations or sessions

Technical Information

When you use our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address

  • Device information
  • Website activity
  • Cookies and analytics data
  • General location data

4. Sensitive Information

As our services involve emotional conversations, you may voluntarily share sensitive personal information during sessions.

We treat all information shared with care, respect, and confidentiality.

Sensitive information will only be processed:

  • With your consent
  • To provide our services
  • Where required by law

5. How We Use Your Information

We may use your information to:

  • Provide listening support services
  • Respond to enquiries
  • Manage bookings and appointments
  • Improve our services and website
  • Send updates or important communications
  • Maintain safety and security
  • Meet legal obligations
  • Send marketing communications where consent is given

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

6. Confidentiality

We aim to provide a private and safe environment for all conversations.

Information shared with us will be treated confidentially unless:

  • There is a serious risk of harm to yourself or others
  • Safeguarding concerns arise
  • Disclosure is legally required
  • Criminal activity is disclosed

Where appropriate, we will attempt to discuss this with you first.

7. Lawful Basis for Processing

Under UK GDPR, we process personal information under the following lawful bases:

  • Consent
  • Legitimate interests
  • Contractual necessity
  • Legal obligations

8. Data Storage and Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from:

  • Unauthorised access
  • Loss
  • Misuse
  • Disclosure
  • Alteration

While we aim to protect your information, no online system can ever be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Data Retention

We only retain personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Maintain business records

When no longer required, information will be securely deleted or anonymised.
