What This Is

Sometimes people don't need fixing...

They just need someone who will truly listen. The Listening Lounge is a confidential online space designed for emotional release — where you can speak freely without fear of judgement.

Truly Heard

Speak freely about whatever is on your mind. No interruptions, no fixing — just pure, attentive listening.

Confidential

Your sessions are private and secure. What you share stays between us — full discretion, always.

Non-Judgemental

No advice given unless asked. No diagnosis. No labels. Just a human being holding space for another.

Flexible Timing

Book a 35 or 45-minute session that fits your schedule. Limited slots available each week.

The Process

How a Session Works

1

Book Your Slot

Choose between a 35-minute or 45-minute session. Pick a time that works for you from the available weekly slots. Secure payment via PayPal confirms your booking.

2

Join the Session

Connect online via video call at your scheduled time. Find a quiet, comfortable space where you feel safe to open up.

3

Speak Freely

This is your time. Share whatever is weighing on you — frustrations, fears, sadness, confusion, or simply the need to be heard. I listen. I hold space. No fixing required.

4

Check-In & Signposting

Before we close, I'll check in on how you're feeling. If needed, I can gently signpost you to appropriate support or services that may help beyond our session.

Your emotions deserve a safe place to land

The Listening Lounge was born from a simple truth: too many people carry their emotions in silence because they have nowhere to release them. This is that space.

My role is to listen — fully, openly, and without judgement. To hold space for whatever you need to express. And when our time together ends, to check in and, if helpful, point you toward further support.
