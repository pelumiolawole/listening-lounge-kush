The Process

How a Session Works

1

Book Your Slot

Choose between a 30-minute or 45-minute session. Pick a time that works for you from the available weekly slots. Secure payment via PayPal confirms your booking.

Calendar scheduling
2

Join the Session

Connect online via a meeting link at your scheduled time. You can opt for audio only or video. Video is not compulsory, so you can choose whatever feels most comfortable for you. Find a quiet, comfortable space where you feel safe to open up.

Audio or video, your choice
3

Speak Freely

This is your time. Share whatever is weighing on you: frustrations, fears, sadness, confusion, or simply the need to be heard. I listen. I hold space. No fixing required.

Uninterrupted listening
4

Check-In & Signposting

Before we close, I'll check in on how you're feeling. If needed, I can gently signpost you to appropriate support or services that may help beyond our session.

Supportive guidance
